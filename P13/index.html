Mr. Wrinkle & His Allergies

SCENARIO

Mr. Wrinkle struggles with quite a few allergies and recently suffered a reaction from his usual drink and snack due to a newly updated ingredients list that now contains soy–he’s allergic to soy! Wrinkle hasn’t had luck in finding drinks or bites that accommodate his allergies while also being tasty and has been on the prowl ever since.

ACTOR

Wrinkle

MOTIVATOR

Recent allergic reactions to his drinks and snacks.

INTENT

Finding an allergy-friendly spot that doesn't sacrifice taste.

ACTION

Wrinkle visits the site for treats, narrowing his search down to sips and bites that accommodate his allergies, are 100% certified safe to eat, and have a transparent ingredients list

RESOLUTION

Mr. Wrinkle selects tasty allergy-safe treats catered to his allergies with confidence, and places his order for pick-up, thrilled to now be able to safely and confidently enjoy some tasty bites without worrying about dealing with an allergic reaction!
